# Problem Statement

## Background

Healthcare appointment management in many hospitals is still handled through manual or semi-digital processes. Patients often experience long waiting times, appointment conflicts, poor communication, and delayed emergency response.

Doctors and hospital staff also struggle with maintaining patient records, scheduling appointments efficiently, and notifying patients about appointment confirmations.

These challenges reduce operational efficiency and negatively impact patient satisfaction.

---

## Existing System

The traditional appointment management process suffers from several limitations:

- Manual appointment scheduling
- Long patient waiting times
- No intelligent prioritization of emergency cases
- Poor communication between hospital and patients
- Limited automation
- No centralized dashboard for monitoring appointments and healthcare data
- High administrative workload

---

## Proposed Solution

The AI Healthcare Appointment Management System was developed using ServiceNow App Engine Studio to automate hospital appointment management.

The application provides:

- Patient Management
- Doctor Management
- Appointment Scheduling
- Rule-based Severity Classification
- Automatic Emergency Prioritization
- Email Notifications
- Platform Analytics Dashboards
- Automated Workflows using Flow Designer

The system significantly reduces manual effort while improving appointment efficiency and healthcare management.

---

## Key Objectives

- Automate healthcare appointment scheduling
- Improve patient experience
- Reduce administrative workload
- Prioritize emergency patients automatically
- Send automatic appointment confirmation emails
- Monitor hospital operations through dashboards
- Centralize healthcare data within ServiceNow
